Season 4 - Story Overview

Season 4 of *Scissor Seven* brings Seven’s journey closer to the ultimate revelation of his past. With memories flooding back and old foes returning, Seven must face the reality of his former life. The season explores themes of redemption, friendship, and the internal struggle between who he was and who he wants to be.

New alliances are formed, and old enemies return to challenge Seven, leading to some of the most intense battles of the series. Thirteen, Dai Bo, and other allies continue to play crucial roles as they help Seven confront powerful adversaries.

As Season 4 unfolds, it becomes clear that Seven’s quest is more than just about his memories—it’s about forging a new path. The season concludes with a thrilling setup for the next chapter, leaving fans eagerly awaiting what lies ahead in his journey.
