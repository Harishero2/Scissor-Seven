Scissor Seven - Story Overview

*Scissor Seven* is a Chinese animated series that follows the story of Seven, a quirky, scissor-wielding assassin with amnesia. His goal is to recover his lost memories while undertaking odd jobs and missions on Chicken Island.

Although Seven works as an assassin, his kind-hearted nature and unusual approach often get him into comedic situations. As he dives deeper into the assassin world, he learns more about his mysterious past.
